Daan Kemper

About

Hello, my name is Daan Kemper app/web devolper student based in the Netherlands.

I develop and design apps, websites and web-apps. Coding allows me to use my creativity and imagination, with that i try to create new unique websites.

Web development

During my study i create various websites as assignments, in my eyes are the assignments too simple for so i started multiple projects for my self to learn and to get faster and smarter in writing code. In my web based projects i mainly use HTML, CSS, Javascript, PHP/Python/C#. For native android apps i use Java and Kotlin and soon ill begin with Swift for iPhone/iPad apps. I also have experience with Progressive Web Apps frameworks: Ionic and PhoneGap. Links to developed apps, Progressive Web Apps and websites created by me you can find on the Work page.

Me

As you may already know by now i like to program. I really enjoy video editing my drone images and other specific requests, i always wanted to make a movie with friends but i did not happen yet. I am only 18 so there lays a whole life of excitement in front of me.
